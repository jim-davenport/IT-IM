What is the IT/Interactive Media Program?

The IT/Interactive Media program at Bridgeport High School gives students a hands‑on, creative, and career‑focused introduction to the world of technology. Whether students are interested in computers, design, programming, or digital media, this program builds real‑world skills that prepare them for college, technical training, or entry‑level industry certifications.

Students explore a wide range of technology fields through engaging, project‑based coursework, collaborative learning, and practical problem‑solving. From building PCs to designing websites and producing multimedia content, the program encourages students to think creatively and work like true IT professionals.

Who We Are?

We are students who create awesome digital media... images, photos, graphics, videos, animations, audio, effects, websites. We also implement and support personal computer systems - both hardware and sofware. We also earn college credit while in high school - for free!

What Courses Do We Take?

  • Digital Design Techniques
  • Personal Computers - CompTIA A+ Certification Prep
  • Web Design
  • Video and Sound
  • Intro to Programming Concepts
